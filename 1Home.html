About Events

As a leading adult sports club, we aim to create a home for the all games in the heart of the city. We have the highest capacity of any adult clubs in the city. We offer a number of ways to play which means you can learn to play any sport and you can do it at any level. We are proud to offer a variety of options from which you can choose, including a Premier League-style matchday experience, a club or matchday program with an on-site trainer, and a youth squad. We also have a Youth League team, a Youth Academy and a Youth Training Academy.
