Computing Student's Society (CSS)

To connect Computer Information Systems and Computer Science students to on-campus resources as well as professionals in the tech industry. Creating an opportunity to cultivate our programming, networking, and career skills.

Art Club

Have a casual creative space dedicated to working on personal and collaborative art projects. Sharing ideas and creative processes with others. We aim to create an open-minded platform for students to cultivate artistic curiosity.

Filipino Students' Association

Provide members, as well as the community, with a taste of Filipino culture. We strive to help its members realize the best aspects of being Filipino-Canadians, while engaging a variety of people and reaching out to the community.

Game Design Club

We are a group of individuals with the aspiration of designing games and dedicated to the creation of video games. We host discussions, events, and meetings. New members are welcome to join and participate.

Outdoors Club

Goal is to provide students at Mount Royal University with access to outdoor adventures and learning opportunities. We assist people in enjoying the environment in a sustainable and environmentally friendly manner.

French Club

We are an inclusive social club that provides opportunities to celebrate, practice, and learn about French language and cultures. All levels of French are welcome, from complete beginners to fluent bilinguals! Come make new friends, work on another language, and experience a different culture!
